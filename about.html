THE WEEKLY WIN

 

Dynamic Messaging on PWS has launched in HK!

 

PWS team has successfully launched the first set of dynamic messages on the home page to deliver personalised and relevant content to our customers.

Curious how it works?

1.    Open https://www.hsbc.com.hk 

2.    Visit a loans related page

3.    Go back to https://www.hsbc.com.hk 

4.    View loans related content on the home page*

*there is a 10% chance that you see the default content – they’re A/B testing

Special thanks to all the hard-working colleagues in the PWS-team, Digital, Optimisation, Testing, UX/Copywriting, Acquisition, PA and VADM, who have helped to make this happen!
